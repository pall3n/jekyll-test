Passengers flying out of Heathrow Airport who want to save themselves the time and trouble of locating a parking space can make use of the special Purple Parking Long Stay Plus service. This professionally operated service is available to all terminals at Heathrow Airport and has won several awards for excellence over the years.

People who book this service will be given details of where to meet a company chauffeur when they arrive at their required Heathrow Airport terminal. The chauffeur will be easy to recognise as they will be wearing a black cap and jacket as well at the distinctive Purple Parking logo and ID tag.

After you leave, your vehicle will be professionally parked by the chauffeur at Purple Parking’s secure compound. This car park has been given the Park Mark Award for safer parking. This prestigious award is only given to compounds that pass certain checks and regularly pass strict checks. The latest security technology is in place as checks are also conducted round the clock by a team of security staff to ensure that nothing happens to your vehicle while you are away.
